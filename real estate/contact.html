FAQ

How to cancel booking?

To cancel a booking, just log in, go to 'My Bookings,' and select the booking to cancel. Follow the prompts, and you're done! Need help? Contact support.

When will I get the possession?

You will typically receive possession within the agreed-upon timeframe outlined in your contract. For specific details regarding your property, please refer to the terms of your agreement or contact the seller directly for updates on the possession timeline.

Where can I pay the rent?

Rent payments can be made online through our website or app. Just log in, go to 'Payments,' and follow the prompts. Need help? Contact support.

How to contact with the buyers?

To contact buyers, click on their profile or listing, or use the 'Contact Seller' button on the property page. Need help? Reach out to support.
